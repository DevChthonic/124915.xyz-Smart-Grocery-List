Why This Tool Exists

My partner and I were tired of disorganized shopping lists scribbled on paper. I wanted a tool that would automatically group "apples" with "bananas" and "milk" with "cheese" to make shopping trips faster. This app is the result: a smart list that organizes itself so you don't have to.
